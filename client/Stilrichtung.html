Stilrichtungen

Realistic

The style of realism describes an art movement that aims to depict reality as accurately and faithfully as possible.

Photographic

In photography, refers to a recognizable group of features that appear in a variety of photographs and reflect a particular visual or conceptual approach.

Minimalistic

Everything superfluous is removed to create a clear and concise structure.

Surrealistic

Surrealist works are often rich in symbols that refer to the unconscious and inner world of the artist.

Cinematic

The chosen means create a certain aesthetic and atmosphere that distinguishes the film from others. These include the visual design, the emotional impact, and the narrative style.

Portrait

In art, a portrait style refers to the different ways in which a portrait can be created, both in painting and photography.
