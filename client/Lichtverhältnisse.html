Lichtverhältnisse

Golden Hour Lighting

is the time shortly after sunrise and shortly before sunset when the sunlight is soft, warm and reddish.

Studio Light

the light refers to the use of artificial light sources in a photographic studio to create images.

Soft Light

Soft light is a type of lighting created by a diffuse light source that does not cast harsh shadows on the subject

Ambient Light

Ambient light is the general lighting of a room that provides sufficient brightness to allow easy orientation and movement.

Backlighting

Backlighting in photography refers to a lighting technique where the main light source is positioned behind the subject, illuminating it from the back.
